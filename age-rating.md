**Age Suitability Statement**

IssieDocs is rated **9+** on the App Store and is designed to be safe for children and students of all ages.

**Intended Audience**

This app is built for pupils with special needs and their teachers. It is developed in by the Beit Issie Shapiro center for technological accessibility in cooperation with SAP Labs Israel.

**No External Content**

The app does not display any external websites, advertisements, or third-party content. All content shown within the app is created by the user on their own device. The app's About screen includes a link to the Beit Issie Shapiro organization website for users who wish to learn more.

**Analytics**

The app uses Google Analytics to collect anonymized usage data (such as which features are used). No personally identifiable information is collected.

**Sharing**

The app allows users to share documents they have created. Sharing is always initiated by the user: when sharing, the app presents the options available on the device — such as email apps, WhatsApp, save to file, and others — and the user selects the method they wish to use. No data is transmitted automatically. The sharing feature is intended to allow pupils to send their completed work to teachers, or for teachers to distribute materials.

**User Feedback**

The app includes an optional feedback form where users can submit a title, description, and optionally their email address. Feedback is sent directly to the development team and is not posted publicly anywhere.
